Nucleate sulfuric acid into aerosol particles, using a power-\/law dependence, for time {\ttfamily del\+\_\+t}. 

The modeled emission rate is $ J = K H^2 $, where $H$ is the concentration of $ \rm H_2SO_4 $ and $K$ is a constant coefficient.

The reference is\+:

C. Kuang, P. H. Mc\+Murry, A. V. Mc\+Cormick, and F. L. Eisele (2008), Dependence of nucleation rates on sulfuric acid vapor concentration in diverse atmospheric locations, {\itshape J. Geophys. Res.}, 113, D10209, doi\+:\href{http://dx.doi.org/10.1029/2007JD009253}{\tt 10.\+1029/2007\+J\+D009253}.
